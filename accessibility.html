Accessibility & Inclusivity

Making travel in Myanmar open and welcoming for all.

Our Commitment

At Burmese Compass, we believe every traveler deserves the chance to experience Myanmar’s beauty. We are committed to creating inclusive, respectful, and safe journeys.

Information for Guests with Disabilities

We provide guidance on destinations and accommodations with accessibility features, and our team is ready to assist with mobility or health-related needs whenever possible.

Diverse Representation

We celebrate diversity in our team, our stories, and our imagery — representing travelers of different ages, backgrounds, and abilities.

Need Assistance?

If you have specific accessibility needs, please contact us. We will do our best to customize your journey and make Myanmar travel safe and enjoyable for you.
