Santorini, Greece

The Crown Jewel of the Aegean Sea

Discover the Magic

Perched on volcanic cliffs overlooking the deep blue Aegean Sea, Santorini captivates visitors with its iconic white-washed buildings, stunning sunsets, and rich ancient history. This enchanting Greek island offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure, from exploring archaeological sites to savoring world-class wines in dramatic clifftop settings.

Must-Visit Attractions

Signature highlights across Myanmar

Bagan Temple Plain

Thousands of ancient pagodas scattered across the plain. Sunrise rides and quiet e-bike trails make it unforgettable.

Inle Lake

Floating gardens, stilt villages, and the iconic leg-rowing fishermen on mirror-still water.

Shwedagon Pagoda

Yangon’s golden stupa glowing at dusk. Spiritual, lively, and the heart of local devotion.

Ngapali Beach

Palm-lined bays, clear water, and fresh seafood. Myanmar’s easygoing coast at its best.

U Bein Bridge

Sunset over the world’s longest teak bridge near Mandalay. Photogenic and timeless.

Golden Rock (Kyaiktiyo)

A gravity-defying gilded boulder perched on a cliff. A revered pilgrimage site with sweeping views.

Essential Travel Information

Helpful notes for planning your Myanmar journey

Best Time to Visit

November to February offers cooler, dry weather. March to May is hot; June to October brings monsoon rains.

Weather & Climate

Tropical monsoon climate. Pack breathable clothing, a light jacket for evenings, and rain protection in wet season.

Currency & Language

Myanmar Kyat (MMK). Burmese is widely spoken; English is common in major destinations and hotels.

Entry & Visas

Most travelers require a valid passport and visa (often eVisa). Check your nationality’s requirements before booking.

Local Tips & Cultural Insights

  • Dress modestly at temples; remove shoes and socks before entering.
  • Carry small cash for markets and donations at pagodas.
  • Sunrise and sunset are the best times for photography in Bagan.
  • Hire licensed local guides for deeper stories and access.
  • Try local dishes like mohinga, tea-leaf salad, and Shan noodles.
  • Respect local customs; ask before photographing people.
